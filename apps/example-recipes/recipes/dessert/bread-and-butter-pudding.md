# Bread and Butter Pudding

Bread and butter pudding is exactly the sort of old-school dessert that deserves a place in a recipes example: humble ingredients, a warm oven, and a result that feels far better than the effort suggests.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 20 minutes
- Cook: 40 minutes
- Serves: 6

## Ingredients

- [ ] 8 slices of bread
- [ ] soft butter
- [ ] raisins
- [ ] 3 eggs
- [ ] 500ml milk
- [ ] 100ml cream
- [ ] 60g sugar
- [ ] nutmeg

## Method

- [ ] butter the bread and cut into triangles
- [ ] layer the bread in a buttered baking dish with raisins between the layers
- [ ] whisk eggs, milk, cream, sugar, and nutmeg together
- [ ] pour the custard over the bread and leave it to soak briefly
- [ ] bake until set in the middle and browned on top
- [ ] serve warm

## Notes

- Brioche makes this especially rich, but ordinary sandwich bread still works.
- A spoonful of custard or cream is never a bad idea.
