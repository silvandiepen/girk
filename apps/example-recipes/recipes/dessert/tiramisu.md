# Tiramisu

Tiramisu is one of those desserts that feels slightly more elaborate than it is. Once the coffee is made and the cream is whipped, the rest is just layering and waiting.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 30 minutes
- Chill: 6 hours
- Serves: 8

## Ingredients

- [ ] 300ml strong coffee
- [ ] 3 eggs
- [ ] 100g sugar
- [ ] 500g mascarpone
- [ ] 250g savoiardi biscuits
- [ ] cocoa powder

## Method

- [ ] cool the coffee completely
- [ ] whisk the yolks with sugar until pale
- [ ] fold mascarpone into the yolk mixture
- [ ] whisk the egg whites separately and fold them through gently
- [ ] dip the biscuits quickly in coffee and layer them in a dish
- [ ] spread over half the mascarpone mixture and repeat
- [ ] chill until set, then dust with cocoa

## Notes

- Tiramisu is better the next day than the day it is made.
- A splash of marsala in the coffee is traditional if you want it.
