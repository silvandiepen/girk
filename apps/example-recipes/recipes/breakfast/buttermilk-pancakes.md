# Buttermilk Pancakes

These are classic diner-style pancakes: soft in the middle, golden at the edges, and sturdy enough for plenty of butter and syrup.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 10 minutes
- Cook: 20 minutes
- Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- [ ] 300g plain flour
- [ ] 2 tbsp sugar
- [ ] 2 tsp baking powder
- [ ] 1 tsp baking soda
- [ ] 1/2 tsp salt
- [ ] 2 eggs
- [ ] 500ml buttermilk
- [ ] 60g melted butter
- [ ] extra butter for the pan

## Method

- [ ] whisk the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl
- [ ] beat the eggs with the buttermilk and melted butter
- [ ] fold the wet ingredients into the dry until the batter is just combined
- [ ] rest the batter for 5 minutes
- [ ] cook ladlefuls in a buttered pan until bubbles appear, then flip and cook until golden
- [ ] stack and serve warm

## Notes

- Serve with maple syrup and a knob of salted butter.
- A handful of blueberries can be folded into the batter for the last few pancakes.
