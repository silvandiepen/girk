# French Toast with Cinnamon Sugar

This is the kind of french toast that feels closer to a proper breakfast plate than a quick snack, especially with berries or stewed fruit on the side.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 10 minutes
- Cook: 15 minutes
- Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- [ ] 8 thick slices of brioche or white bread
- [ ] 3 eggs
- [ ] 200ml milk
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla extract
- [ ] 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- [ ] 2 tbsp sugar
- [ ] butter for frying

## Method

- [ ] whisk the eggs, milk, vanilla, half the cinnamon, and half the sugar together
- [ ] dip each slice of bread in the custard and let the excess drip off
- [ ] fry in butter over medium heat until browned on both sides
- [ ] mix the remaining cinnamon and sugar together
- [ ] dust the hot slices with cinnamon sugar before serving

## Notes

- Day-old bread gives the best texture.
- A spoonful of berry jam on the side works well if syrup feels too sweet.
