# Baked Salmon with Dill Potatoes

This dish keeps the method simple but still feels like a proper sit-down dinner, especially when the potatoes are tossed warm with butter and dill.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 15 minutes
- Cook: 30 minutes
- Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- [ ] 4 salmon fillets
- [ ] 800g baby potatoes
- [ ] 200g green beans
- [ ] butter
- [ ] fresh dill
- [ ] lemon
- [ ] olive oil
- [ ] salt and black pepper

## Method

- [ ] boil the potatoes until tender
- [ ] bake the salmon with olive oil, salt, pepper, and lemon slices
- [ ] steam or boil the green beans until just tender
- [ ] toss the potatoes with butter and chopped dill
- [ ] plate the salmon with potatoes and beans

## Notes

- A spoonful of mustard stirred into the butter makes a quick sauce.
- This works well with cod too if salmon is not on hand.
