# Fish and Chips

Fish and chips is best when it stays straightforward: crisp batter, flaky fish, and chips that are hot enough to steam when you break them open.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 20 minutes
- Cook: 35 minutes
- Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- [ ] 4 white fish fillets
- [ ] 200g plain flour
- [ ] 1 tsp baking powder
- [ ] 250ml cold sparkling water
- [ ] 1kg potatoes
- [ ] oil for frying
- [ ] lemon wedges
- [ ] tartar sauce

## Method

- [ ] cut the potatoes into thick chips and fry or roast until crisp
- [ ] mix flour, baking powder, and sparkling water into a light batter
- [ ] dust the fish with flour
- [ ] dip each fillet in batter and fry until deeply golden
- [ ] drain on paper and season at once
- [ ] serve with chips, lemon, and tartar sauce

## Notes

- Keep the batter cold for the lightest finish.
- Malt vinegar on the chips keeps the dish firmly in old-school territory.
