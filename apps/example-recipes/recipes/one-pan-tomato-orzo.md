# One-Pan Tomato Orzo

A weeknight recipe that keeps everything in one pan.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] 3 garlic cloves
- [ ] 250g orzo
- [ ] 1 can crushed tomatoes
- [ ] 750ml vegetable stock
- [ ] olive oil
- [ ] parmesan
- [ ] basil

## Steps

- [ ] soften the onion in olive oil
- [ ] add garlic and stir for 1 minute
- [ ] add orzo and toast lightly
- [ ] pour in tomatoes and stock
- [ ] simmer until the orzo is tender
- [ ] finish with parmesan and basil

## Serve With

- [ ] green salad
- [ ] extra parmesan
