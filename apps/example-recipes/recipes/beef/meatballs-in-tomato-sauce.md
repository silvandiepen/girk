# Meatballs in Tomato Sauce

This is the sort of recipe that feels useful all year: meatballs over pasta, tucked into a roll, or served with a spoonful of polenta.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 20 minutes
- Cook: 35 minutes
- Serves: 4 to 6

## Ingredients

- [ ] 500g minced beef
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] breadcrumbs
- [ ] grated parmesan
- [ ] 2 garlic cloves
- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] 1 tin chopped tomatoes
- [ ] olive oil
- [ ] parsley

## Method

- [ ] mix the beef with egg, breadcrumbs, parmesan, and half the garlic
- [ ] roll into small meatballs
- [ ] brown the meatballs in olive oil and set aside
- [ ] cook the onion and remaining garlic until softened
- [ ] add chopped tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes
- [ ] return the meatballs to the sauce and cook through gently
- [ ] finish with parsley

## Notes

- Do not overcrowd the pan when browning the meatballs.
- The sauce can be loosened with pasta water if serving over spaghetti.
