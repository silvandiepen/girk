# Beef Stew

Beef stew should taste settled rather than rushed. Given enough time, the broth thickens, the vegetables soften, and the whole pot tastes like it belongs on a winter table.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 25 minutes
- Cook: 2 hours 15 minutes
- Serves: 6

## Ingredients

- [ ] 900g stewing beef
- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] 3 carrots
- [ ] 500g potatoes
- [ ] 2 tbsp flour
- [ ] 500ml beef stock
- [ ] 1 tbsp tomato paste
- [ ] Worcestershire sauce
- [ ] thyme

## Method

- [ ] brown the beef in batches
- [ ] soften the onion and carrots in the same pot
- [ ] stir in flour and tomato paste
- [ ] return the beef and add stock, Worcestershire sauce, and thyme
- [ ] simmer gently until the beef is tender
- [ ] add potatoes for the final stretch of cooking
- [ ] season and serve hot

## Notes

- The stew is even better the next day.
- Crusty bread or mashed potatoes both work well underneath.
