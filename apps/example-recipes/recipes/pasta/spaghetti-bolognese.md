# Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti bolognese belongs in a classic recipe example because almost everyone understands what good versions of it should taste like: slow, rich, and deeply savoury without being fussy.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 20 minutes
- Cook: 1 hour 20 minutes
- Serves: 6

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] 2 carrots
- [ ] 2 celery stalks
- [ ] 3 garlic cloves
- [ ] 500g minced beef
- [ ] 2 tbsp tomato paste
- [ ] 1 tin chopped tomatoes
- [ ] 250ml beef stock
- [ ] 400g spaghetti
- [ ] parmesan

## Method

- [ ] cook the onion, carrots, and celery until softened
- [ ] add garlic and minced beef and cook until browned
- [ ] stir in tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes
- [ ] add tomatoes and stock, then simmer gently until thick and rich
- [ ] boil the spaghetti in salted water
- [ ] toss the pasta with sauce and serve with parmesan

## Notes

- The sauce improves if it rests for 10 minutes before serving.
- A splash of milk or cream can soften the acidity at the end.
