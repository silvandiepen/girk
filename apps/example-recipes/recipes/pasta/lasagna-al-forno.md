# Lasagna al Forno

Lasagna is more work than weeknight pasta, but it pays you back by feeding a table generously and reheating beautifully the next day.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 35 minutes
- Cook: 1 hour 15 minutes
- Serves: 8

## Ingredients

- [ ] 12 lasagna sheets
- [ ] 500g minced beef
- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] 2 garlic cloves
- [ ] 2 tbsp tomato paste
- [ ] 1 tin chopped tomatoes
- [ ] 700ml béchamel sauce
- [ ] 150g grated mozzarella or parmesan

## Method

- [ ] brown the beef with onion and garlic
- [ ] add tomato paste and chopped tomatoes and simmer until thick
- [ ] spoon béchamel into the base of a baking dish
- [ ] layer pasta sheets, meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese
- [ ] repeat until the dish is full, finishing with béchamel and cheese
- [ ] bake until bubbling and deeply golden on top
- [ ] rest for 15 minutes before slicing

## Notes

- Resting the lasagna is what gives you neat slices instead of a soft collapse.
- A green salad on the side is enough to make this a full Sunday meal.
