# Chicken Parmesan

Chicken parmesan is comforting in a very direct way: a crisp breaded cutlet, rich tomato sauce, and enough cheese to pull everything together.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 25 minutes
- Cook: 30 minutes
- Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 large chicken breasts, halved horizontally
- [ ] flour
- [ ] 2 eggs
- [ ] breadcrumbs
- [ ] grated parmesan
- [ ] 1 jar tomato sauce
- [ ] mozzarella
- [ ] olive oil

## Method

- [ ] flatten the chicken pieces lightly
- [ ] coat each piece in flour, egg, and breadcrumbs mixed with parmesan
- [ ] fry until golden on both sides
- [ ] place in a baking dish with tomato sauce spooned over
- [ ] top with mozzarella and bake until bubbling
- [ ] serve with spaghetti or a green salad

## Notes

- Frying first keeps the coating crisp under the sauce.
- A little basil on top freshens up the richness.
