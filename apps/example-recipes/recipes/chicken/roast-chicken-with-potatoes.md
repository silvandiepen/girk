# Roast Chicken with Potatoes

Few dishes make a house smell better than roast chicken. This version is simple on purpose: one bird, one tray, and potatoes that crisp in the chicken fat.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 20 minutes
- Cook: 1 hour 30 minutes
- Serves: 4 to 6

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 whole chicken
- [ ] 1.2kg potatoes
- [ ] 1 lemon
- [ ] 4 garlic cloves
- [ ] butter
- [ ] olive oil
- [ ] thyme or rosemary
- [ ] salt and black pepper

## Method

- [ ] season the chicken well inside and out
- [ ] stuff the cavity with lemon and garlic
- [ ] toss the potatoes with oil, salt, and herbs in a roasting tray
- [ ] sit the chicken on top and rub the skin with butter
- [ ] roast until the skin is deep golden and the juices run clear
- [ ] rest the chicken before carving and turn the potatoes in the tray juices

## Notes

- Start the potatoes in hot water for 8 minutes first if you want extra crisp edges.
- Save any leftovers for sandwiches or soup.
