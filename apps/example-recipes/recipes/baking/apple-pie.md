# Apple Pie

Apple pie belongs in any old-fashioned recipe collection. It is familiar, generous, and still one of the best things to put on a table after a roast dinner.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 35 minutes plus chilling
- Cook: 50 minutes
- Serves: 8

## Ingredients

- [ ] pastry for a double-crust pie
- [ ] 6 large apples
- [ ] 120g sugar
- [ ] 1 tsp cinnamon
- [ ] 1 tbsp plain flour
- [ ] 20g butter
- [ ] 1 egg for glazing

## Method

- [ ] roll out half the pastry and line a pie dish
- [ ] peel and slice the apples
- [ ] toss the apples with sugar, cinnamon, and flour
- [ ] pile the filling into the dish and dot with butter
- [ ] cover with the top pastry and crimp the edges
- [ ] brush with beaten egg and cut a few steam holes
- [ ] bake until the crust is golden and the filling is bubbling

## Notes

- Let the pie cool a little before slicing or the filling will spill out.
- Custard, cream, or vanilla ice cream all work.
