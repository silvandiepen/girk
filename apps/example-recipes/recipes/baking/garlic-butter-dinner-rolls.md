# Garlic Butter Dinner Rolls

These rolls are built for the middle of the table, where people keep tearing one off until the tray is suddenly empty.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 25 minutes plus rising
- Cook: 20 minutes
- Serves: 12 rolls

## Ingredients

- [ ] 500g strong white flour
- [ ] 7g instant yeast
- [ ] 1 tsp sugar
- [ ] 1 tsp salt
- [ ] 300ml warm milk
- [ ] 50g melted butter
- [ ] 2 garlic cloves
- [ ] parsley

## Method

- [ ] mix the flour, yeast, sugar, and salt
- [ ] add warm milk and melted butter and knead until smooth
- [ ] leave to rise until doubled
- [ ] divide into 12 rolls and place in a buttered tray
- [ ] leave to rise again until puffy
- [ ] bake until golden
- [ ] brush with melted garlic butter and parsley while warm

## Notes

- These are best eaten the day they are baked.
- They make a good side for soup, roast chicken, or pasta night.
