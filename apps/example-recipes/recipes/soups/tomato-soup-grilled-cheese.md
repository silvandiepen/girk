# Tomato Soup with Grilled Cheese

Tomato soup on its own is fine. Tomato soup with a buttery grilled cheese sandwich is dinner.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 15 minutes
- Cook: 35 minutes
- Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] 2 garlic cloves
- [ ] 2 tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 2 tins chopped tomatoes
- [ ] 500ml chicken or vegetable stock
- [ ] 2 tbsp cream
- [ ] 8 slices sandwich bread
- [ ] cheddar cheese
- [ ] butter

## Method

- [ ] soften the onion in olive oil until sweet and translucent
- [ ] add garlic and cook briefly
- [ ] stir in the tomatoes and stock, then simmer for 25 minutes
- [ ] blend until smooth and finish with cream
- [ ] butter the bread, fill with cheddar, and fry until the sandwiches are deep golden
- [ ] cut the sandwiches in halves and serve beside the soup

## Notes

- A pinch of sugar helps if the tomatoes are sharp.
- Cheddar and a little mustard make the sandwich feel more like a proper diner-style toastie.
