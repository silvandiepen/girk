# Chicken Noodle Soup

Chicken noodle soup earns its place in any recipe archive because it is dependable, forgiving, and exactly the sort of dish people return to without needing a special occasion.

## Quick Look

- Prep: 20 minutes
- Cook: 45 minutes
- Serves: 6

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] 2 carrots
- [ ] 2 celery stalks
- [ ] 2 garlic cloves
- [ ] 1 tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 1.5L chicken stock
- [ ] 2 cooked chicken breasts, shredded
- [ ] 180g egg noodles
- [ ] parsley
- [ ] salt and black pepper

## Method

- [ ] soften the onion, carrots, and celery in olive oil
- [ ] add garlic and cook for 1 minute
- [ ] pour in the stock and simmer until the vegetables are tender
- [ ] add the shredded chicken and noodles
- [ ] cook until the noodles are soft
- [ ] finish with parsley and season well

## Notes

- Leftover roast chicken works especially well here.
- If the noodles sit too long in the pot, add a splash of hot stock before serving again.
