# Crispy Chickpea Wraps

A compact lunch recipe that works well as a checklist-driven page.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 wraps
- [ ] 1 can chickpeas
- [ ] smoked paprika
- [ ] cumin
- [ ] yogurt
- [ ] lemon
- [ ] lettuce
- [ ] cucumber

## Prep

- [ ] dry the chickpeas well
- [ ] season with paprika, cumin, salt, and oil
- [ ] roast until crisp
- [ ] mix yogurt with lemon and salt

## Assemble

- [ ] warm the wraps
- [ ] spread the yogurt sauce
- [ ] add lettuce and cucumber
- [ ] add crispy chickpeas
- [ ] fold and serve
